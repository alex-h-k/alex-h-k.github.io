About Me

I’m Alex and I’m from China. I’m currently living in Christchurch and have been here for five years. I see Christchurch as my second hometown. Living in New Zealand gives me the a clear image of diversity. It’s awesome that People from different countries are living together here with respecting each other’s culture. I’m proud of my identity as an open minded Chinese. Getting to know the history of different countries and their culture really fascinates me, that’s why I travel a lot when I have time. I also like adventure, like outdoor activities. Riding quad bike across shallow rivers, hiking and camping in nature, skiing are always my activity list on weekend. In terms of value, in social aspect I think the way we treat people should be the way we want to be treated. We are all living on the same planet as a big community. People should help each other to make the world a better place. The reason I came to study in Dev-academy is seeking for another career.
